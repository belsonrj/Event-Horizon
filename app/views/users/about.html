About Event Horizon

Overview

Event Horizon is an app that let's you index the collection of artists you've seen live as well as the venues you've visited. When creating a new artist/venue you can choose to attach an already existing artist/venue or create a new one at the same time. This function links the artist and venue to create a more dynamic collection. Clicking on an artist or venue will show you what venues you've seen this artist at, or conversely what artists you've seen at this venue.

Venue Rating

Another interesting function for Event Horizon is the rating of venues. A venue can be rated along four main categories. Clicking the 'Rated Venues' page will break down your rated venues and show you which venues top each category for you.

  • Layout: How easy or difficult is it to maneuver through this venue? Are there bottleneck points or areas where view is substantially obstructed?

  • Sound: How is the sound in this venue? Are sound levels consistent? Acoustics?

  • Comfort: If there's seating: is it comfortable? If not: is the standing area clean or spacious?

  • Prices: Are drinks/concessions outrageously expensive or affordable?

  • Comments: Add any notes, comments, or tips about this venue that you'd like to record.

Artist Meeting

If you're like me; you might want to meet the band. Maybe get a signature, picture, or just say "Hi". Checking this box on the Artist create or edit screen will log this into your profile. You can then view your "Met Artists" list to keep track.

Event Tracking

The 'Upcoming Events' section is a separate portion of the app where you can add upcoming events that are saved and shared to the app where you and other users can view and comment.
